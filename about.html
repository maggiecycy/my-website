Who I Am

Hi, I'm Maggie, a second-year Computer Science student with a passion for exploring technology and turning ideas into real projects. I love learning new programming languages, experimenting with web development, and diving into data analysis and AI applications.

I also actively contribute to open-source projects, constantly learning from the community, and aim to merge technical expertise with practical solutions that can make life better.

Feel free to explore my projects and reach out if you'd like to connect or collaborate!
